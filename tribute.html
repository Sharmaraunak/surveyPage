    Here is a timeline of his Life

  • 1932 -Born on 17 August 1932 in Chaguanas, Trinidad and Tobago
  • 1939 -Naipaul's family moved in with them in a big house in Trinidad's capital, Port of Spain. Naipaul enrolled in the government-run Queen's Royal College, a well-regarded school that was modelled after a British public school.
  • 1951 -Naipaul won a Trinidad Government scholarship that allowed him to study at any institution of higher learning in the British Commonwealth; he chose Oxford.
  • 1952 -Naipaul met Patricia Ann Hale, his future wife, at a college play. With Hale's support, he began to recover and gradually to write.She became a partner in planning his career.
  • 1954 -He began appearing on the BBC radio programme Caribbean Voices once a week.
